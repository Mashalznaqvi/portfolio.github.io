Personal Takeaways

My journey with the Fast-Dine app has been an incredible learning experience. Throughout this process, I encountered numerous challenges and made valuable mistakes, which served as lessons that propelled my growth. Interacting with users and uncovering their issues was both the most challenging and exhilarating part of this journey. While I take pride in the final product, I recognize areas for future improvement, including:

Enhanced Advertising: I aim to diversify the way we showcase special deals, promotions, and discount coupons to engage users more effectively.

Expanded User Interviews: Conducting additional user interviews will be a priority, as they are instrumental in unearthing new challenges and enhancing the overall performance of the app.

Creative Design Elements: In the future, I plan to introduce captivating visuals and animations into the app's design to maintain user engagement and enhance the overall user experience.
